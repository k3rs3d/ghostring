Joining

Your site need only be devoid of bigotry & ads, and you should be willing to add the webring links.

Add the following webring HTML somewhere on your site (I simply put my links in the footer):

<a href="https://ghostring.neocities.org/YOUR_SLUG/previous">‚Üê</a>
<a href="https://ghostring.neocities.org/">Ghostring</a>
<a href="https://ghostring.neocities.org/YOUR_SLUG/next">‚Üí</a>

(Replace YOUR_SLUG with a keyword representing your site - for example, slug kersed for the website kersed.net)

My webring application generates pages which automatically redirect visitors to the sites adjacent to each other in the list. This way, the links connect us all together in a loop!

It's okay if you want to customize the links - changing the arrow characters to something else, or using images/buttons instead of plain text, etc. The important thing is to allow visitors to easily find their way between sites!

Updating the List

Once you have the links on your page, there are several ways to get on the list:

  • leave a comment on neocities!
  • submit a pull request!
  • message me on fedi!

Required Information

When sending your request, the only required details are your site's URL, and the slug (site keyword) that you picked. I need the slug in order to activate your links.

Please specify the URL where you put the links so visitors can find them. If your links are on your front page, then your normal URL will do. If your links are elsewhere on your site - like on a special "links" page - then please use that specific page as your URL.

That's it! If you've added the links & submitted a request, then you're all set!

Optional Information

Optionally, you can also include additional information for your site listing:

  • Full title of your site
  • A brief description of your site
  • Some form of contact info (email, fedi, neocities, a contact page on your site, etc)
  • Your RSS link, if you have one

None of the above is required; it only helps flesh out your entry in the directory.

Updates

Please understand it may take a few days (or more) to process any requests and update the webring!

The ringfairy application doesn't run automatically right now. I have to run it by hand to re-generate the webring every once in a while. It's not the kind of webring software that detects changes on-the-fly, rather, it's a simple HTML generator. So if I'm busy or away from my computer for any reason, it may take some time before I can update the webring again. At the bottom of this page, there should be a timestamp showing the last time the webring was updated. In the spirit of the small web, thanks for your patience!

If your site still doesn't appear on the webring even after being added to the Ghostring list, then most likely ringfairy wasn't able to automatically detect the required links on your site. This is a necessary verification step and I can't accomodate sites that omit the webring links, or sites that put them inside any dynamic content which can't be easily scraped. The links must be present in plain HTML.

Also, Ghostring uses a shuffle mode. Every time the webring is regenerated, the sequence is randomized. That means each update will scramble everyone's links. Some sites naturally get more traffic than others. Shuffling the webring helps balance out the traffic. So check back in on the webring once in a while to discover some cool new sites!

Leaving

If Ghostring links can't be found on your site at the time the webring is re-generated, then it won't be able to be included for that iteration of the webring. After a while, if the links don't get fixed after many attempts, I'll drop your site from the list in order to maintain the list.

If you want to remove yourself from the Ghostring, it'd be appreciated if you gave me a heads up so I can update the list! Otherwise, simply remove the links from your site. Eventually it will get removed.
